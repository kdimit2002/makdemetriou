Who We Are

Supporting Chefs. Elevating Kitchens. Delivering Excellence.
Since 1996, MAK Demetriou has been a reference point for professional catering equipment in Cyprus. Driven by innovation, reliability, and expertise, we design and deliver solutions that transform every professional kitchen.
  • We thoroughly analyse each partner’s needs, providing customised solutions for every project.
  • We deliver projects that enhance efficiency and give our clients a distinct advantage in quality.
  • We collaborate with leading, innovative, and reliable manufacturers from around the world.
  • We empower professionals to automate and optimise their daily kitchen operations.
Our main goal is to offer our partners the opportunity to unleash their creativity and deliver exceptional experiences to their customers.
MAK Demetriou: Trust, expertise, and true care for every food service professional.
